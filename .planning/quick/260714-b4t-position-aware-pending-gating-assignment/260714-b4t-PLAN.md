<objective>
Two fixes observed live by the product owner:
1. The "Action required" forcing modal for step 2 (Assign Designer/Architect for Brief, requiredPosition=head_designer) appears for ANY design/architect-role user — it must only appear for the Head Designer. Root cause: getMyWork's pending filter and the header switcher's mine/youract signals check role (canActOnGraphStep) and the assignee gate (gatedToUserId), but never step.requiredPosition. Server-side authorizeStep already enforces position (strict equality, fetched fresh from DB) — this is a visibility/nagging bug, not an authorization hole.
2. Newly created projects have NO deadlines on the early steps, so the (incoming) countdown timer has nothing to count. Per the owner's spec: at project creation, auto-stamp deadlines for Assign Designer/Architect (+1 day), Brief Taking (+2 days), and Invoice & Delivery Timeline (+2 days), all measured from creation. Later steps' deadlines get set at the merged step 4 wizard, unchanged.
</objective>
