<objective>
Fix the Materials / Accessories Readiness checklist (DB slug `factory_manager_readiness`) so photo evidence is gated per item on the yes/no answer, Material and Accessories are mandatory to answer, and the last item (Upholstery) is optional overall (both to answer and to photograph).

**Purpose:** Remove the incorrect "attach 3 photos" requirement that currently blocks submission on the last step, and replace it with two correct rules: (1) one photo per item, required only when that item is answered "yes"; (2) Material and Accessories must be answered before the checklist can advance/submit, while Upholstery may be skipped entirely (unanswered, or answered with a photo if "yes").

**Output:** Two shared pure validation helpers (`missingConditionalPhotos` and `missingRequiredAnswers`) consumed by both the client wizard and the server action, per-item photo uploaders in the wizard scoped to this one checklist, and server-side enforcement of both rules.

---

## ⚠ Discrepancy surfaced (read before executing)

The task brief states "every OTHER item in this checklist already correctly requires exactly 1 photo attached." This is NOT reflected in the codebase. The actual current behavior of `factory_manager_readiness`:

- Its 3 items ("Material control readiness form attached", "Accessories readiness form attached", "Upholstery readiness form attached") are seeded all on `step: 1` (`scripts/migrate-v2-production-pipeline.ts:150-154`, `seedChecklist` at :156-177). The wizard's single-step fallback (`checklist-wizard.tsx:69-71`) renders them one-item-per-page.
- Photos are collected in ONE bulk block that only appears on the LAST step (`checklist-wizard.tsx:206`), requiring `REQUIRED_PHOTOS['factory_manager_readiness'] = 3` (`lib/workflow.ts:296`) photos for the whole checklist, stored flat in `checklists.photoData`.
- The wizard and submit action IGNORE the per-item `isPhotoRequired` schema flag. There is currently NO per-item photo capture for any checklist.

So the fix must INTRODUCE per-item, answer-gated photo capture — it does not exist yet. To respect the "do not touch any other checklist type" constraint, this new behavior is scoped to `slug === 'factory_manager_readiness'`; every other slug keeps its exact current behavior (production_process still requires its 1 bulk photo; delivery/installation still collect none).

**Scoping decision (confirmed by user):** The photo requirement is gated purely on the answer being "yes" ("single photo gated on yes/no answer"). Answering "no" does not force a photo. Separately, and in addition to the photo rule, Material and Accessories are now **mandatory to answer** — the checklist cannot advance past their step or be marked complete/submitted while either is unanswered. Upholstery stays fully optional: it can be left unanswered and the checklist still submits; if answered "yes" it follows the same 1-photo rule as every other item. Answer-required enforcement for every other checklist type is left unchanged (the wizard does not force answers on any other slug today).

**Identifying Material/Accessories vs. Upholstery:** Template items have no stable machine key today (`checklist_template_items` has no `key`/`code` column — see `scripts/migrate-v2-production-pipeline.ts:150-154`), only `label`, `sortOrder`, `step`. Distinguish the optional item by label: the seed label is exactly `'Upholstery readiness form attached'` (migrate-v2-production-pipeline.ts:154). Match case-insensitively on `label.toLowerCase().includes('upholstery')` so the rule survives minor label edits. Everything else under `factory_manager_readiness` (currently Material, Accessories) is mandatory. This matching lives in one place (`lib/workflow.ts`) and is reused by both the answer-required helper and anywhere else that needs to tell the items apart.
