<objective>
Fix inconsistent step-completion feedback on `yes_no_upload` and `approval` workflow steps (10 of 23 live steps, ~43%). Both currently render success and error messages identically (gray `text-sm text-gray-700`, no auto-redirect), so a rejected submission (e.g. "You cannot approve your own submission…") reads like a success.

Apply the EXISTING proven pattern from `assignment-step.tsx`: green `text-green-700` with a leading `✓` on success, red `text-error` on failure, and a ~1.4s auto-redirect to the dashboard after the step actually completes. Do NOT invent a new mechanism.

Also fix the secondary issue in `app/(app)/workflow/step/page.tsx`: unauthorized-role (lines 47-49) and position-mismatch (lines 57-59) currently `redirect()` silently — surface a short message using the wording already used in the graph actions.

Purpose: Prevent PMs from misreading a rejected submission as a completed step.
Output: Consistent, color-coded, redirecting feedback across all three fulfillment kinds.
</objective>

- Render: `{message && <p className={\`text-sm ${ok ? 'text-green-700' : 'text-error'}\`}>{message}</p>}`
- Component takes a `redirectTo?: string` prop; page passes `redirectTo={dashboard}`
